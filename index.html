My name is Abdullah Sahebi, and I am currently residing in the beautiful city of Herat. Later, I pursued my secondary and high school education in Herat, where I graduated from Inqelab-e-Islami High School. In 2021, I took the Kankor test in Herat and successfully passed it. Currently, I am pursuing my degree in Computer Science at Herat University, which has been an exciting and enriching experience so far.
